# Yard-Privacy-Policy
Privacy Attestation for the Yard App

  **Last updated: March 28, 2026**

  ## Overview

  Yard ("the App") is a private, building-restricted marketplace and community platform available exclusively to verified residents of participating
  buildings. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

  ## Information We Collect

  ### Information You Provide
  - **Name and unit number** — collected when you join a building
  - **Email address** — collected via your account login
  - **Listing content** — photos, descriptions, and details of items you post
  - **Messages** — content of conversations between residents
  - **Community board posts** — content you post to your building's board

  ### Information Collected Automatically
  - **Device push notification token** — to send you message notifications
  - **Usage data** — basic app activity to maintain and improve the service

  ## How We Use Your Information

  - To verify your residency and provide access to your building's community
  - To facilitate listings, messaging, and community posts between residents
  - To send push notifications for new messages
  - To maintain and improve the App

  ## Third-Party Services

  Yard uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  - **Clerk** (authentication) — [clerk.com/privacy](https://clerk.com/privacy)
  - **Ably** (real-time messaging) — [ably.com/privacy](https://ably.com/privacy)
  - **Expo** (push notifications) — [expo.dev/privacy](https://expo.dev/privacy)
  - **Neon** (database hosting) — [neon.tech/privacy](https://neon.tech/privacy)

  ## Data Sharing

  We do not sell your personal information. Your data is only shared:
  - With other verified residents of your building (name, unit number, listings, messages)
  - With the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the App

  ## Data Retention

  Your data is retained while your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal information and content will be removed from our
  systems.

  ## Deleting Your Account

  You can delete your account directly within the App:

  1. Open the **Profile** tab
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Account**
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

  This will permanently remove your account and all associated personal data, including your listings, messages, and community posts. This action
  cannot be undone.

  You can also request account deletion by emailing **yardappsupport@gmail.com** with the subject line "Delete My Account".

  ## Children's Privacy

  Yard is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

  ## Changes to This Policy

  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the App. Continued use of the App after
  changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

  ## Contact Us

  If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request data deletion, contact us at:

  **yardappsupport@gmail.com**
